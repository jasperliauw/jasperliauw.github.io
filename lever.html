More facts about lever

The longer the lever, the higher you can lift an object. In a type 1 lever, the fulcrum (pivot) is between the object to be moved and the effort. A see-saw is a very simple type 1 lever. Pliers, a hammerâ€™s claw and even scissors are also type 1 levers. Scissors are really two levers joined at the fulcrum. In a type 2 lever, the load is between the fulcrum and the effort.
